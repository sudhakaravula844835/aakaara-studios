Vesica Piscis

Two overlapping circles creating a vesica piscis — an ancient sacred geometry symbol meaning "creation" and "form." Directly maps to "Aakaara" (form/shape). The emphasized overlap represents the meeting of two stories. Minimal, intellectual, timeless.

Heritage

Devanagari Abstract

Abstracted from the Devanagari character "आ" (Aa) — the first syllable of Aakaara. Not literally readable, but the shirorekha (headline bar), vertical strokes, and flowing curves carry the calligraphic spirit. A cultural signature that non-readers see as elegant abstraction.
